  Throughout my time in junior high, high school, and undergraduate experiences I have done a great deal of self-studying to extend my knowledge and technical skills. I've only just begun to record these experiences here for you to see! Feel free to check out my personal thoughts & notes. By far the most extensive resource I've used is MIT OpenCourseWare, a platform where MIT's professors can post lecture notes, lecture videos, and other materials to support freedom of education. Without MIT OpenCourseWare, I feel my personal growth would have been stunted; so let this be a personal thank you and recommendation for the platform.
